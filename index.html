    Exercise

    One of the most efficient ways to prevent obesity is to exercise. However, there's an alarming trend in which less and less Americans are getting adequte exercise on a daily, or even weekly, basis. In fact, several NIH studies have determined a causal link between a lack of exercise and heart disease in the US. As a result, the number cause of preventable death in the United States is heart disease.
